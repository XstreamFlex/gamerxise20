Our Story

GamerXise was created out of necessity by a founder with ADHD and a TBI survivor who was suffering from chronic migraines. Struggling to maintain healthy exercise habits, he turned to what he knew worked: the immersive power of video games. By merging gaming and exercise, he found a way to improve his health and happiness. GamerXise is now a solution designed to benefit the world, offering full-body workouts that can be done while playing your favorite games, improving both body and mind.
